# Phase 1 Usage Guide

## Getting started

Sign in with Google or an email magic link at the login screen. You are taken straight to the map, centred on San Francisco by default.

---

## Modes

Switch modes using the toolbar at the bottom of the screen.

### Browse
Default mode. Pan and zoom the map freely. Tap any polaroid pin to open it.

### Draw
Map panning is locked. Drag your finger or mouse across any area to paint cells you have visited. Each cell is roughly half a city block.

- Painted cells appear in teal over the map.
- At higher zoom levels (zoomed out), cells are grouped into larger parent hexagons automatically.

### Erase
Same as draw but removes painted cells. Drag across any painted cell to unpaint it.

### Pin
Tap anywhere on the map to drop a photo memory. A dialog opens where you can:
- Choose a photo from your camera roll or file picker.
- Add an optional caption.
- Save the pin.

Photo pins appear as polaroid-style cards floating on the map.

---

## Undo / Redo

The `↩` and `↪` buttons undo or redo your last draw or erase strokes within the current session.

Keyboard shortcuts:
- `⌘Z` (Mac) / `Ctrl+Z` (Windows) — undo
- `⌘⇧Z` / `Ctrl+Shift+Z` — redo

Undo history clears when you reload the page.

---

## Stats

The top-left panel shows how many cells you have painted and how many photos you have pinned.

---

## Signing out

Click the small "out" button in the top-left stats panel.

---

## Installing as a home screen app

On iPhone: open in Safari, tap Share, tap **Add to Home Screen**.
On Android: open in Chrome, tap the browser menu, tap **Add to Home Screen** (or **Install app**).
